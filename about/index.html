About · built by a Type 1 diabetic

Built by someone who’s been where you are.

LocalDiabetic isn’t built by a company looking at a market. It’s built from lived experience, for real people — with a promise you can check yourself.

“

I’m a Type 1 diabetic, and I call it a gift. It lives with me 24/7 — and I’ve won big at this life.

I’ve been through the surgeries and the hospital stays, and come through every one of them standing. Diabetes is a 24/7 job — I know what it’s like to come home and find everything scattered across a drawer, an inbox, and ten different apps.

So I built the thing I needed — and the thing you need — to win too. A calm place for your records, reminders that actually find you, and a Bee that understands diabetes, all on a little box in your own home. Nothing leaves your house.

My rule is simple: give people what they need, not what you have. And it costs you nothing.

— Donovan · Type 1 Diabetic · building LocalDiabetic one day at a time
