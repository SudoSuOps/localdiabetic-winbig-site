The ownership win

𝕏  Post on X ✉  Forward to a friend ▶ Watch on YouTube

The big win isn't "AI."

The big win is ownership.

A small local appliance in your home can become your private diabetes vault:

  • Your records.
  • Your reminders.
  • Your supplies.
  • Your questions.
  • Your care notes.
  • Your trends.
  • Your next appointment list.

Not scattered across apps. Not lost in emails. Not forgotten between visits. Just sitting there quietly, helping you stay ready.

Because diabetes doesn't only happen at the doctor's office. It happens at breakfast. At bedtime. At the pharmacy. At the kitchen table. At 2 a.m. when something feels off.

LocalDiabetic is being built around that simple idea:

Keep the important stuff close. Keep it private. Make tomorrow easier.

That's the edge appliance win.
