🐝 The little box

One small box. Your whole diabetic life — and it never leaves home.

LocalDiabetic doesn't run in someone else's cloud. It runs on a small, quiet computer you own — sitting on a shelf at home. Your records, your reminders, and a diabetic-smart AI all live on that box. Here's what runs it, what it really costs, and why owning the box is the whole point.

What this compute represents

It's not a gadget. It's ownership.

A cloud health app charges you every month and keeps your data. The little box is the opposite: you pay once for hardware you own, and your records plus the AI live on it, in your house. The only thing that ever leaves is a non-private receipt — never your health data.

That one-time box buys something a subscription never can: a diabetic-smart brain that answers without sending your life to a stranger's server — and keeps working when the internet doesn't.
